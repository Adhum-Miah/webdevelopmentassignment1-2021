However, I had issues with creating the hamburger and linking it to my navigation bar but it is still responsive. I also had issues with creating the colums in order to have the necessary layout for the Portfolio, CV and Site Report Pages. I hope I am able to learn how to resolve these issues in order for me to be more successful in the next assignment

On the whole, I like the module and Thomas is a really nice guy and is good at his job which I appreciate.
